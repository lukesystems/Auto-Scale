# AutoScale Positioning

## Final positioning

AutoScale is a trend-aware short-form video distribution loop for SaaS founders.

It starts from a product URL, studies proven video patterns, creates short-form video experiments, publishes through connected accounts, tracks what brings users, and compounds the winners.

AutoScale is not a generic AI video generator or a generic content calendar.

## Hero copy

**Badge:** AI short-form growth agent for SaaS founders

**Headline:** Crack distribution with videos that compound.

**Subheadline:** AutoScale finds proven short-form video patterns in your niche, turns them into TikToks, Reels, and Shorts for your product, tracks what brings users, and compounds the winners.

**Primary CTA:** Start Your First Growth Run

**Secondary CTA:** See how it works

**Support line:** Find what works → Ship videos → Track users → Compound winners

**Positioning line:** Built for SaaS founders who need distribution, not another content calendar.

## Messaging rules

- Lead with the learning loop, not generation volume.
- Lead with the founder's real anxiety: failing to crack repeatable distribution.
- State that volume only works after the format works.
- Make the product URL the clear starting point.
- Describe evidence as public, stored, and confidence-scored.
- Separate observed video evidence from strategic inference.
- Explain why each video exists and what signal it should test.
- Describe Postiz as the publishing layer, not the intelligence layer.
- Treat autopilot as rules-controlled and explicitly enabled.

## Psychological promise

Founders are not primarily afraid of making videos. They are afraid that they will never find a repeatable way to bring users to the product.

The landing page should move the founder from:

```txt
What should I post?
```

to:

```txt
This format brings users. Make more variants.
```

Distribution is not luck. It is a loop that gets sharper through evidence, iteration, and compounding winners.

## Final product direction

Preserve the existing evidence chain and Growth Run spine. Improve the product from its current state rather than building a parallel video demo:

```txt
source evidence
→ pattern
→ strategy
→ video concept
→ script and storyboard
→ rendered video
→ approval
→ scheduled or exported video
→ tracked user or revenue signal
→ learning
→ winner variant
```

Near-term work should verify and harden the existing runtime, deepen VideoTrend evidence, improve video quality, and make tracking and compounding affect the next Growth Run. Do not replace Postiz, broaden into account farming, or make AI influencers the main wedge.
